We’re opening a new location in the United States!

Heading type 2

IGS GeboJagema is proud to introduce its advanced facility, IGS Peachtree City, located near Atlanta, Georgia, USA. This state-of-the-art mold facility embodies our commitment to precision, excellence, and technological advancement. Equipped with innovative engineering, manufacturing, and inspection tools, alongside comprehensive service and repair capabilities, IGS Peachtree City is ready to surpass the most demanding specifications.

Our facility offers a complete range of mold care services aimed at enhancing efficiency, reducing downtime, and prolonging equipment life. Our experienced team is ready to assist you, offering support both at our IGS facility and on-site, ensuring tailored solutions for your needs.
